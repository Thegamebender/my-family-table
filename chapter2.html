The Enigma of the whispering forest

Chapter 2

Evelyn traced the symbols on the altar, her fingertips tingling. The moon emerged from behind clouds, casting silver threads upon her face.
She closed her eyes, and the forest sang—a chorus of memories woven into the very fabric of existence.
The first secret revealed itself: “To find love, seek the forgotten path.” Evelyn’s heart quickened.
She vowed to unravel the forest’s riddles, to follow the winding trail even when it led into darkness.
The second secret came as a warning: “Beware the shadowed glade where time unravels.” Evelyn shivered.
She had heard tales of travelers lost in that timeless pocket, their laughter echoing for centuries.
And the final secret—the one that would change her fate: “To break the enchantment, sacrifice what you hold dearest.”
Evelyn hesitated. What did she cherish most? Her family? Her dreams? Or perhaps the memory of a stolen kiss under the harvest moon?
